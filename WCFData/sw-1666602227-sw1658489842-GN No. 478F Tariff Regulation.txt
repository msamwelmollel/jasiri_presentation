THE WORKERS COMPENSATION ACT, (CAP. 263)

REGULATIONS

(Made under section 94)

THE WORKERS COMPENSATION (PAYMENT OF TARIFF) (AMENDMENT) REGULATIONS, 2022

Citation and Commencement

These Regulations may be cited as the Workers Compensation (Payment of Tariff) (Amendment) Regulations, 2022 and shall be read as one with the Workers Compensation (Payment of Tariff) Regulations, hereinafter referred to as “principal Regulations” and shall come into effect on the 1st day of July, 2022.
Amendment of Regulation 5

The principal Regulations are amended in regulation 5(2) by deleting the words “zero point six per centum (0.6%)” and substituting for them the words “zero point five per centum (0.5%)”.
