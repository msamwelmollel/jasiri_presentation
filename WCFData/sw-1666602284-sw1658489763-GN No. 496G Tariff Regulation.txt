Workers Compensation (Payment Of Tariff)

GOVERNMENT NOTICE NO. 496G published on 30/6/2021

THE WORKERS COMPENSATION ACT, (CAP. 263)

REGULATIONS

(Made under section 94)

THE WORKERS COMPENSATION (PAYMENT OF TARIFF) (AMENDMENT) REGULATIONS, 2021

Citation and Commencement

1.-(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Workers Compensation (Payment Tariff) (Amendment) Regulations, 2021 and shall be read as one with the Workers Compensation (Payment Tariff) Regulations, hereinafter referred to as the “principal Regulations”.

(2) These Regulations shall come into operation on the 1st day of July, 2021.

Amendment of Regulation 5

The principal Regulations are amended in regulation 5(2) by deleting the words “one per centum (1%)” and substituting for them the words “zero point six per centum (0.6%)”.
